About Little Lemon

Welcome to Little Lemon, where culinary excellence meets warm hospitality. Nestled in the heart of Cologne, our restaurant offers an unforgettable dining experience crafted by a passionate team of four: Alireza Baghban, Kateryna Yakovleva, Ilona Vichnevskaia, and Goda Sai Keerthi Chikkala. Together, we bring a shared love for food and innovation to your plate.

Our Story

At Little Lemon, we believe every meal should tell a story. Our team came together with a vision to create a space where traditional recipes meet modern flair. Drawing inspiration from global cuisines and combining our diverse expertise, we’ve crafted a menu that celebrates fresh, seasonal ingredients and bold flavors. Whether you’re craving a comforting schnitzel or an exciting Mediterranean dish, every bite at Little Lemon is made with care and passion.

Our journey began with a shared dream of offering not just food but an experience. From a cozy ambiance to carefully curated dishes, Little Lemon reflects our dedication to excellence. Each of us brings a unique perspective, ensuring that your time with us is nothing short of exceptional.

Why Dine with Us?

At Little Lemon, we strive to make every visit special. Here’s why our guests keep coming back:

  • A menu that blends traditional favorites with creative twists.
  • Fresh, locally sourced ingredients that bring out the best flavors.
  • A welcoming, rustic ambiance perfect for any occasion.
  • Dedicated chefs and staff who prioritize your dining experience.

Our Vision

Our mission is simple: to create a community around food. Whether you’re dining with us in Cologne, exploring our recipes online, or enhancing your cooking with our specialty sauces, we aim to make gourmet dining accessible and enjoyable for everyone. At Little Lemon, it’s not just about food—it’s about connection, celebration, and the joy of sharing a meal.

Thank you for choosing Little Lemon. We’re excited to welcome you to our table and share our love for great food with you!
